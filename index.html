About me

I am now a first year M.Eng. student from School of Electronic and Computer Engineering at Peking University, supervised by Prof. Yuexian Zou. During my B.S. journey, I served as a Research Intern since April 2019, supervised by Prof. Yue Wu, as well as since March 2021, supervised by Prof. Jingqiao Zhang.

Currently, my research interests include:

  • Knowledge graph: empowering and landing Industries.

  • Federated learning: privacy preservation in Finance and representational learning in Healthcare.

  • Unstructured data processing: sensitive information extraction.

  • Point cloud processing: model segmentation and reconstruction.

  • Social network analysis: uncovering the result-oriented factors of teamwork and applying them to the real world.

NOTE: I am now actively seeking for an opportunity to be a PhD candidate starting 2025 (preferably outside China mainland) or for potential job positions, focusing on knowledge graphs, search recommendations, etc. If you have any related opportunities, I’d really appreciate it if you could reach me out via email.
